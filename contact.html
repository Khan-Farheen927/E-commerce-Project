Common Questions

Quick answers so you don’t have to wait for a reply.

How long does delivery take? We ship within 48 hours of order confirmation. Standard delivery takes 3—5 business days across India. Express delivery (1—2 days) is available at checkout for select pin codes.
Do you ship internationally?Not yet — but we’re working on it. International shipping is planned for early 2026. Sign up for our newsletter to get notified first.
Are your products true to size?ThreadCo tees and hoodies are oversized by design. If you prefer a more fitted look, we recommend sizing down. Check the Size Guide on any product page for exact measurements
How do I track my order?How do I track my order? Order Tracking page.
Can I collaborate or submit designs?Absolutely. ThreadCo works with independent Indian artists on every drop. If you have a design concept or want to collab, reach out via the form above and select “Collaboration / Partnership” as the subject. We read every single message.
